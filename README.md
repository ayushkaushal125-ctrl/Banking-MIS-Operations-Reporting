## Banking MIS & Operations Reporting

## Objective
To create structured MIS reports for monitoring banking operations,
productivity, and SLA adherence.

## Background
This project is based on experience in BFSI banking backend operations,
including account settlements, closures, and international process handling.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- MIS Reporting
- Operations Tracking

## Reports Covered
- Daily & Monthly MIS
- Volume Tracking
- Productivity Reports
- SLA Performance Monitoring

## Outcome
Demonstrates banking domain knowledge, operations reporting,
and analytical decision-making skills.
